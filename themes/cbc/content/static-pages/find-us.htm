The church is in the village of Crockenhill, about a mile outside Swanley. We're about 5 minutes by car from Junction 3 of the M25, and Junction 1 of the M20. There is a small car park at the church, with disabled access to both halls. There is plenty of extra car parking on the streets near the church.

The 477 bus from Orpington to Dartford stops in the village, only a minute or two's walk from the church. Swanley train station is a 15 minute walk away.
